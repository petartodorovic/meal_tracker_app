## Product Summary

The app helps users define a body composition goal, calculate daily macronutrient targets, log meals, and understand whether they are on track for the day and over time.

The core goal is not just food logging. The app should make the user's nutrition status legible at a glance: calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, and progress toward a selected mode:

- Build/Bulk: put on weight with a controlled calorie surplus.
- Shred/Cut: lose weight with a controlled calorie deficit.
- Maintain: hold weight and improve consistency.
